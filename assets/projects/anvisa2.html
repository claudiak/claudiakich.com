2014 | ANVISA - Patient Safety
Web Design / UX / Joomla / Mobile
As a expert consultant I was responsible for requirements interviews, information architecture, design, development, tests and training.

In this project the challenge was to reconcile the interests of the sectors involved with the best interest of the target audience.

Given the large number of documents, I categorized the content according to their themes and importance and then I implemented the content management Joomla.

Another challenge was to train 10 employees to use the CMS. I prepared manuals and video tutorials, in addition to 40 hours of classroom training.

Due to the complexity of the technical terms used by the institution, I also fix the terminology.
